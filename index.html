Behind the Platform

I am Gunnar Bersi, a security professional with over a decade of comprehensive experience across the security sector. My career spans a wide spectrum of roles—from security officer and monitoring station operator to technician, sales representative, security consultant, and junior business manager.

This diverse background has equipped me with a holistic understanding of both the operational and strategic facets of security systems, bridging the gap between on-the-ground implementation and high-level client advisory.

Alongside my industry expertise, my background in programming empowers me to merge practical security knowledge with technical innovation. Currently, I am a proud team member at Nortek Security Solutions, contributing to the advancement of modern security infrastructure.

I developed this platform to revolutionize how security systems are design and planned. By enabling professionals to seamlessly integrate and visualize equipment directly onto architectural blueprints, this tool is built to drive accuracy, enhance efficiency, and elevate overall project execution.
